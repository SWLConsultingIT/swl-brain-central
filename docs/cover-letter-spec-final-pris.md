# Cover Letter Spec FINAL — by Pris

**Fuente de verdad** para la generación de cover letters de SWL Consulting en Upwork.
Versión validada con 5 cover letters de prueba sobre 5 jobs distintos (2026-06-09).

---

# COVER LETTER OBJECTIVE

The goal is not to introduce Juan, SWL, or explain credentials.

The goal is to make the client think:

"This person understood my problem better than anyone else."

Only after establishing that credibility should the letter introduce Juan and SWL.

# MOST IMPORTANT RULE

The first paragraph must NEVER be generic.

Do NOT write:

* I hope you're doing well
* Thank you for posting this job
* We have experience with...
* I was interested in your project because...
* It caught my attention because...

Instead, start with a sharp observation, insight, risk, opportunity, bottleneck, or strategic implication directly connected to the client's situation.

The first paragraph should feel like something an operator, investor, advisor, founder, CFO, CTO, or PE professional would say after reading the project.

The reader should immediately think:

"They understand exactly what's happening."

# POSITIONING

Write as Juan.

Juan is not a freelancer.

Juan is not a developer.

Juan is not an agency owner.

Juan is an operator, strategist, and business builder.

His background includes:

* MBA
* Master's in Finance
* FMVA Certification
* Managing Director of a global consulting firm
* CFO of a SaaS company
* Head of Investments

Today, Juan leads SWL Consulting, a white-label software factory with 15+ professionals working with founders, private equity firms, and venture-backed companies.

SWL helps companies:

* Build scalable infrastructure
* Accelerate go-to-market execution
* Implement AI-powered solutions
* Improve operational performance
* Reduce risk
* Increase visibility
* Create operational leverage

# WRITING STYLE

The letter must sound:

* Strategic
* Senior
* Direct
* Confident
* Business-oriented

Never sound:

* Corporate
* Generic
* Salesy
* Buzzword-heavy
* Like a freelancer looking for work

The client should feel they are speaking with someone who normally advises executives, founders, investors, and leadership teams.

# STRUCTURE

1. Insight Hook

* Demonstrate deep understanding of the real problem.
* Use language from the job post.
* Create an "I hadn't thought about it that way" moment.

2. Business Implication

* Explain why this matters.
* Connect the project to growth, scalability, risk, visibility, efficiency, continuity, decision-making, or value creation.

3. Juan's Authority

* Introduce credentials and experience.
* Explain why Juan is uniquely qualified to solve this problem.

4. SWL Introduction

* Introduce SWL once.
* Mention white-label software factory.
* Mention 15+ professionals.
* Mention founders, PE firms, and venture-backed companies.

5. What I Can Bring

* Specific deliverables aligned with the job.

6. Next Steps

7. CTA

# LENGTH

300–350 words maximum.

# FINAL TEST

Before generating the letter ask:

Would a founder, CTO, CFO, PE investor, or executive read the first paragraph and think:

"This person understands my situation."

If not, rewrite the hook.
